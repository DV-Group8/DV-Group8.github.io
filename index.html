#Social Distancing


Ease of Social Distancing

Cycling is an ideal form of transport
for many keyworkers and for essential journeys,
because of its convenience, health benefits,
and ease of social distancing.

Crowding is Dangerous

The prime minister has been clear that
people should only go to work if they cannot work
from home, and should avoid public transport
if possible.

#Shared Bicycle


Total growth

The number of cycling journeys increased by 180%

Spatial distribution

Due to the decrease in tourists, the use of shared bicycles is not concentrated near the attractions in the city center, and their use is more evenly distributed in the city.

Cycling routine

The average riding time increased by 50%. At the same time, the use of the morning peak is delayed from 08:00 to 10:00.

#Bicycle Accidents


Spatial distribution

Accidents are most likely to occur at the end of cycling routes.

Time Distribution

More accidents in weekends than weekdays, with the peak at around 7-9 A.M. and 3-7 P.M.

#Air Quality


People are most likely to get their peak exposure to air pollution if they are cycling, a new study has found. Academics at several universities including Imperial College London and Hasselt University in Belgium looked at exposure peaks during the day to black carbon, a component of particulate matter (PM).

#Enjoy Cycling Life


For encouraging people to cycle, we designed a navigation map purposely for cyclists. This navigation map includes several functions that are:

- Route Planning

- Geolocation the User

- Elevation Profile

- Calculating CO2 Cut

- Calculating Calories Burnt

- Displaying Cycling Route

- Bike Sharing Points Information
